Research

Where I work

Two labs and one project. Here's what I'm working on in each, and the people I get to think alongside.

ChatterLab

University of Chicago · May 2026 — present

WithDr. Marisa Casillas and Subin Kim (2nd-year PhD)

Ongoing work on automated classification of multi-party conversational speech in naturalistic home environments. Building speaker diarisation pipelines in PyTorch and TensorFlow on a large speech corpus. First author on an abstract accepted to ICIS 2026.

Littafin Fasaha

May 2026 — present

WithHajara-Yasmin Isa (project lead)

Littafin Fasaha is an interactive computer science learning platform for Hausa speakers, with the goal of extending to other underrepresented African languages. The aim is to make programming education accessible to learners who hit a language wall with existing English-only resources.

I contribute to LLM fine-tuning and frontend development. We're currently in the second phase, building out the platform itself.

Learning and Language Lab

University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign · Aug 2025 — present

WithDr. Anastasia Stoops, Dr. Jessica Montag, and Dr. Volodymyr Kindratenko (NCSA)

Working on a project titled Understanding Reading Development through Visual and Linguistic Factors using Deep Learning Transformers. We're examining whether deep learning architectures (LSTMs, transformer-based models like BERT) can model and predict the eye movements of teens and adults from visual and linguistic features of text.
